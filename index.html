About Me

My introduction

Hi! I'm Michael, a third year at UC Berkeley, where I triple major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS), Business (as part of the MET Program), and Data Science with an emphasis in Applied Math and Modeling.

I also teach CS 70 and conduct research as a part of the Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) Lab under Professor Anca Dragan. I will be joining Jane Street Capital as a Quantitative Trading Intern next summer.

In my free time, I like to make YouTube videos, work out, play guitar, and challenge people to games of chess!
